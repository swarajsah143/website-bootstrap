Foods for Children Aged 2-5 Years

  1. Fresh Fruits (e.g., apples, bananas, berries, oranges)
  2. Fresh Vegetables (e.g., carrots, broccoli, spinach, bell peppers)
  3. Whole Grain Cereals (e.g., whole wheat, oats, bran flakes)
  4. Low-Fat or Skim Milk (after age 2)
  5. Lean Proteins (e.g., chicken, turkey, lean beef, tofu)
  6. Fish (e.g., salmon, tuna, tilapia)
  7. Legumes (e.g., beans, lentils, chickpeas)
  8. Peanut Butter (if no allergies)
  9. Whole Wheat Bread or Pasta
  10. Brown Rice
  11. Cheese (in moderation)
  12. Yogurt (low sugar)
  13. Eggs
  14. Nuts (if no allergies, and in small, age-appropriate portions)
  15. Water (encourage hydration)
  16. Juices (in moderation, 100% fruit juice without added sugar)
  17. Homemade Smoothies (with fruits and yogurt)
  18. Cottage Cheese
  19. Oatmeal (with fruit and nuts)

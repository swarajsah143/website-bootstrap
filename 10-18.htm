Foods for Children Aged 10-18 Years

  1. Pizza (with whole wheat crust and plenty of veggies)
  2. Grilled Chicken Salad (with mixed greens and balsamic vinaigrette)
  3. Whole Wheat Pasta (with marinara sauce and lean ground turkey)
  4. Fresh Fruits (e.g., apples, bananas, grapes, citrus fruits)
  5. Fresh Vegetables (e.g., broccoli, spinach, kale, bell peppers)
  6. Brown Rice (with stir-fried vegetables and tofu)
  7. Lean Proteins (e.g., salmon, lean beef, turkey burgers)
  8. Oatmeal (with berries and a drizzle of honey)
  9. Quinoa (with roasted vegetables and chickpeas)
  10. Eggs (scrambled, poached, or in omelets)
  11. Low-Fat or Greek Yogurt (with granola and fresh berries)
  12. Whole Wheat Sandwiches (with lean cold cuts and veggies)
  13. Nuts and Seeds (as snacks or in yogurt)
  14. Peanut Butter or Almond Butter (as a spread or in smoothies)
  15. Homemade Smoothies (with spinach, bananas, and protein powder)
  16. Hummus (with baby carrots, cucumber slices, or whole wheat pita)
  17. Cottage Cheese (with pineapple or mixed berries)
  18. Lean Beef or Veggie Burgers (on whole wheat buns)
  19. Whole Grain Cereals (e.g., bran flakes, whole grain cheerios)
  20. Water (encourage hydration)

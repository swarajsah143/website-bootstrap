Foods for Seniors (Aged 70+ Years)

  1. Oatmeal (with sliced bananas and a sprinkle of cinnamon)
  2. Salmon (baked or grilled, rich in omega-3 fatty acids)
  3. Leafy Greens (e.g., spinach, kale, and collard greens, high in vitamins and minerals)
  4. Blueberries (rich in antioxidants and beneficial for cognitive health)
  5. Yogurt (low-fat or Greek, a good source of probiotics and calcium)
  6. Lean Protein (e.g., chicken, turkey, and lean cuts of beef)
  7. Whole Wheat Bread (fiber-rich for digestive health)
  8. Broccoli (a cruciferous vegetable, packed with nutrients)
  9. Brown Rice (a whole grain, beneficial for heart health)
  10. Sweet Potatoes (rich in fiber and vitamins)
  11. Apples (a good source of fiber and antioxidants)
  12. Cottage Cheese (high in protein and calcium)
  13. Carrots (provide beta-carotene for eye health)
  14. Beans (e.g., black beans, kidney beans, for protein and fiber)
  15. Low-Sodium Soup (nutrient-rich and hydrating)
  16. Avocado (healthy fats and potassium)
  17. Whole Grain Pasta (with a tomato-based sauce and vegetables)
  18. Almonds (a heart-healthy snack, rich in vitamin E)
  19. Lean Fish (e.g., tilapia, cod, easy to digest and low in mercury)
  20. Green Tea (rich in antioxidants and potentially beneficial for metabolism)

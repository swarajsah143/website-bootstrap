Foods for Children Aged 5-10 Years

  1. Grilled Cheese Sandwiches (whole wheat bread and low-fat cheese)
  2. Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce (whole wheat pasta and lean ground beef or turkey)
  3. Fresh Fruits (e.g., apples, bananas, grapes, oranges)
  4. Fresh Vegetables (e.g., carrots, cucumbers, broccoli, cherry tomatoes)
  5. Chicken Nuggets (baked or grilled, not fried)
  6. Macaroni and Cheese (whole wheat pasta and low-fat cheese)
  7. Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwiches (on whole wheat bread)
  8. Tuna Salad (with whole wheat crackers or on a sandwich)
  9. Scrambled Eggs (with diced veggies and whole grain toast)
  10. Mini Pizzas (whole wheat English muffins with tomato sauce and cheese)
  11. Yogurt (low sugar and with fresh fruit)
  12. Homemade Smoothies (with yogurt, spinach, and berries)
  13. Oatmeal (with a drizzle of honey and sliced bananas)
  14. Pancakes (whole wheat or whole grain) with fruit toppings
  15. Grilled Chicken Strips (with dipping sauce and a side of veggies)
  16. Hummus (with carrot and cucumber sticks or whole wheat pita)
  17. Cheese and Whole Wheat Crackers
  18. Roasted Turkey and Cheese Roll-Ups (with whole wheat tortillas)
  19. Popcorn (lightly salted or seasoned)
  20. Water (encourage hydration)

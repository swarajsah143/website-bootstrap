MESSAGE FROM PRINCIPAL

"

We extend our warmest welcome to you at National Infotech where we started our journey in the pursuit of educational excellence in B.S 2062. It has been a wonderful experience so far; one that can be cherished by all associated with this institution: the students and the teachers. The entire gamut of activities at NI reflects its objective of nurturing young minds and helping them grow as responsible citizen who can make positive contribution to the society and the nation. To support our programs and teaching we have three science labs, a library and a computer lab equipped with modern facilities. The lecture rooms are equipped with smart boards with interactive teaching material developed at Pearson Education to help in understanding the scientific process through visualization and allow students a 24 hours access to teaching and learning materials.

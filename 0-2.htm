Foods for Children Aged 0-2 Years

  1. Breast Milk or Formula
  2. Pureed Fruits (e.g., apples, bananas, pears, peaches)
  3. Pureed Vegetables (e.g., sweet potatoes, carrots, peas, squash)
  4. Baby Cereal (e.g., rice cereal, oatmeal cereal)
  5. Mashed Avocado
  6. Plain Yogurt (full-fat)
  7. Mashed Banana
  8. Cooked and Pureed Meats (e.g., chicken, turkey)
  9. Cooked and Pureed Fish (e.g., salmon, tilapia)
  10. Cooked and Pureed Legumes (e.g., lentils, chickpeas, black beans)
  11. Oatmeal
  12. Soft Cheese (e.g., cheddar, mozzarella)
  13. Hard-Boiled Egg Yolks (avoid egg whites until around one year old)
  14. Whole Grains (e.g., rice, pasta)
  15. Natural Nut Butters (e.g., peanut butter, almond butter)
  16. Cooked and Pureed Spinach
  17. Cooked and Pureed Broccoli
  18. Whole Milk Yogurt (after the first year)
  19. Water (small sips in a sippy cup as needed)
  20. Homemade Applesauce (unsweetened)
